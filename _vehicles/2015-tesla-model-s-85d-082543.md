## The Driving Experience
The Model S 85D delivers a driving experience unlike any conventional sedan. Press the accelerator and 485 lb-ft of torque responds instantly—no turbo lag, no gear changes, just smooth, relentless thrust that pins you to the seat. The dual-motor AWD system provides confident traction in any weather while maintaining impeccable balance through corners. The low-mounted battery pack creates a remarkably low center of gravity, resulting in flat, composed handling that rivals dedicated sports cars. Inside, the cabin is serene—wind and road noise are the only companions as the electric powertrain operates in near silence. The massive 17-inch touchscreen puts climate, navigation, and entertainment at your fingertips, while over-the-air updates mean the car continues to improve long after you drive it home.
