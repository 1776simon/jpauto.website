## The Driving Experience
The Fusion S Hybrid delivers a refined, composed driving experience wrapped in a sleek midsize sedan. The hybrid powertrain provides immediate torque off the line, making city driving feel effortless as the electric motor assists acceleration from stops. The transition between electric and gasoline power is nearly seamless—you'll rarely notice when the engine engages. On the highway, the Fusion cruises quietly and comfortably, with well-tuned suspension that absorbs road imperfections without feeling floaty. Steering is nicely weighted and accurate, inspiring confidence through corners despite the sedan's efficiency-focused mission. The regenerative braking system takes some initial adjustment but soon becomes intuitive, and many drivers appreciate coasting to a stop with minimal brake pedal use. Inside, the cabin is spacious and quiet, with comfortable seating for five and a modern dashboard layout centered around the SYNC infotainment system.
