## The Driving Experience
The Model 3 Long Range delivers exhilarating performance wrapped in everyday practicality. Plant your foot and 389 lb-ft of instant torque launches you forward with smooth, relentless acceleration—no lag, no gear changes, just pure electric thrust. The dual-motor AWD system distributes power seamlessly between axles, providing confident traction in any weather while enhancing cornering dynamics. Steering is quick, accurate, and well-weighted, responding immediately to every input. Inside, the cabin is serene at speed—without engine noise, only subtle wind and road sounds accompany your drive. The minimalist interior centers on a single 15-inch touchscreen that controls everything, while the all-glass roof floods the space with natural light. With 353 miles of range, range anxiety becomes a distant memory.
