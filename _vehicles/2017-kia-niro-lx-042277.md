## The Driving Experience
The Niro LX redefines what a hybrid can be. Step inside and you'll find a crossover that prioritizes driving enjoyment alongside efficiency. The 6-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers quick, responsive shifts—a welcome departure from the droning CVTs found in most hybrids. Power delivery feels natural and linear, with the seamless handoff between electric motor and gasoline engine barely noticeable. In Eco mode, the Niro maximizes efficiency with featherlight throttle response, while Sport mode unlocks sharper acceleration for highway merging and spirited driving. The elevated seating position provides excellent visibility, and the compact footprint makes city maneuvering effortless. Inside, the cabin is surprisingly spacious, with generous rear legroom and a flat cargo floor when the seats fold. Road noise is well-controlled, and the regenerative braking feels natural rather than grabby. This is a hybrid that doesn't ask you to sacrifice driving enjoyment for fuel savings.
