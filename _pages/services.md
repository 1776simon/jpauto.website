## Complete Automotive Solutions

At JP AUTO, we're more than just a dealership. We offer a full range of automotive services to keep your vehicle running smoothly and looking its best.

### Pre-Purchase Inspection

Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a rigorous 150-point inspection to ensure quality, reliability, and safety. Our certified technicians check:

- **Engine & Transmission** - Complete mechanical assessment
- **Brakes & Suspension** - Safety-critical components
- **Electrical Systems** - All electronics and diagnostics
- **Interior & Exterior** - Condition and functionality
- **Fluid Levels & Leaks** - Comprehensive fluid check
- **Tire Condition** - Tread depth and wear patterns

### Professional Detailing

We provide professional detailing services to keep your vehicle looking showroom-ready:

- **Exterior Detailing** - Hand wash, clay bar, wax, and polish
- **Interior Detailing** - Deep clean, vacuum, shampoo, and conditioning
- **Paint Correction** - Scratch and swirl mark removal
- **Ceramic Coating** - Long-lasting paint protection
- **Headlight Restoration** - Crystal-clear visibility

### Vehicle History Reports

Transparency is our priority. CARFAX reports are available for a fee on any vehicle in our inventory:

- **CARFAX Reports** - Complete vehicle history available for a fee upon request
